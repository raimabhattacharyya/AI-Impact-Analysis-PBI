# AI-Impact-Analysis-PBI
“Global Workforce Intelligence: AI Impact & Opportunity Mapping” explores how Artificial Intelligence and automation are reshaping global employment landscapes. This project analyses job roles, industry growth, salary patterns, automation risks, and diversity metrics to uncover future workforce opportunities and challenges. The insights support businesses, policymakers, and professionals in making informed decisions to stay competitive and future-ready. 
In this project the global job market from 2024 to 2030 is analyzed with a focus on the impact of Artificial Intelligence and automation. Using a dataset of approximately 30,000 job records, the study evaluates industry-wise job demand, salary trends, automation risks, diversity metrics, and future workforce opportunities. The insights support job seekers, organizations, and policymakers in making informed, future-ready decisions.
